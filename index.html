Case 02

Rheumatology Case

A 54-year-old female with symmetrical swelling of MCP and PIP joints. Morning stiffness lasts $>2$ hours.

Lab: Anti-CCP $>200$ U/mL, RF $120$ IU/mL.

✅ 正確答案：A) Rheumatoid Arthritis。

關鍵在於對稱性小關節受累與高特異性的 Anti-CCP。
